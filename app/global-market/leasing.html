Leasing

Texvalley is a one-of-its kind platform for the textile industry. Located right on the Cochin-Bangalore highway, it is also one of India’s largest destination malls.

Spread over a sprawling of 20 lakh sq. ft, Texvalley is South India’s largest textile marketplace, offering fully furnished showrooms for textile manufacturers and retailers to help build extraordinary brands of the future.
