Welcome to Texvalley

Welcome to the platform where everyone thrives in prosperity.

Texvalley, South India's premier destination for textiles, trade and lifestyle. Strategically located on the Cochin-Bangalore highway, Texvalley spans a sprawling expanse of 20 lakh square feet. Over a decade, Texvalley has evolved from a specialised B2B marketplace into a comprehensive B2B and B2C integrated mall.

Our Journey

In 2014, we started our journey as a B2B textile marketplace with an aspiring vision to become South India's first true destination mall. We have continuously expanded and adapted to meet the dynamic needs of our customers. Today, Texvalley is home to over 500 brands representing various segments of the textile and allied industries, with plans to increase this number to 1,500 local and global brands within the next two years.

Our expansion is set to significantly boost footfall, with projections indicating an increase from 60,000 visitors per month to over 500,000 monthly visitors in the near future. This growth is expected to create direct and indirect employment opportunities for over 50,000 individuals, thereby contributing to the regional economy.
